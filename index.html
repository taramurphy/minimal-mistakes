--- layout: single author_profile: true --- I am an Associate Professor and ARC Future Fellow at the University of Sydney. My research is in radio transients, and I co-lead the transients projects on two major SKA pathfinders, the Murchison Widefield Array and the Australian SKA Pathfinder. I teach computational science and data science for physics and astronomy students, and I'm particularly interested in online teaching and teaching innovation. With my other hat on I run an edtech business, [Grok Learning](https://groklearning.com), that provides coding courses for high schools and universities.
